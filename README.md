# Ocio - Skip the search. Start the trip
Ocio is an AI-native travel platform that transforms abstract, vibe-based prompts into comprehensive, 
ready-to-use itineraries. Moving beyond traditional search filters, 
Ocio leverages the **Gemini API** to interpret natural language capturing specific moods, 
aesthetics, and hidden energies to surface destinations that align with a user’s unique travel intent. 
Once a destination is matched, the app generates a curated collection of points of interest, 
including tailored restaurants, landmarks, and local experiences. These are visualized through an interactive, 
map-centric interface, providing users with a seamless transition from a vague "feeling" to a fully-mapped journey. 
Built using **React**, **NextJs** and **Python FastAPI**.
